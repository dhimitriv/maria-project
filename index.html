Who am I?

Hi! My name is Maria

I'm 27 years old and I live in Essex UK. I live with my husband and my baby girl named Natalia.

I've been painting and drawing since I can remember and today I absolutely think that creating art is the best therapy.

Recently I decided to open an Etsy store to share my art with you.

If you like one of my pieces and purchase it you will be much appreciated! Thank you in advance :)
